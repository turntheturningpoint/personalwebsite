The Creator









High! I'm Angelique Gloria, the youngest daughter of my parents and was
born on the 12th of October. I have always been a quite peculiar child.
Having different interests is nothing new, of course, but I find that
very little people around me have the same interests as I do and those
who do are miles away from me and out of reach. I guess I got my head
down in a rabbit hole of fantasy and chaotic things, enjoying every
moment of it, but who can blame me in indulging in these kind of things?
You would've done the same if you wanted to...

I like to make up stories, myths, and epics of how the hero will always
fall and the villain triumphant. Observing my surroundings is one of my
interest, looking at intricate details and intrigued of how they got there.
Coming up with different outcomes and histories of it. Often times, I stare
in space for a numerous amount of time and this is where my creative mind
usually hits. So I welcome you, to have a look at this exhibition of mine!
Be careful to not loose your way...
